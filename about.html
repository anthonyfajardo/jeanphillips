BEHIND THE COLOURS

I am Jean Phillips, an Edmonton artist, who began painting back in 1999 after a life-long love for watercolours. I always have my camera on hand to capture interesting subject matter. I do a lot of florals and other things that catch my eye.

I love using intense, vibrant colour.
